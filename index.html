Features
Everything you need to stay in control
Six built-in capabilities that work together to keep your AI assistants safe, visible, and under your command.

Secure Login

Users sign in through your existing identity provider — Google, Microsoft, Okta, or any standard login system. No separate passwords to manage. Each person is linked to your organization automatically.

Rules & Policies

Decide exactly what your AI assistants can and cannot do. Create allow and block lists for tools, choose how much activity gets logged, and update rules anytime — changes sync to all instances automatically.

üõ°

Tool Control

Every action the AI tries to take is checked against your rules first. If it's not allowed, it gets blocked on the spot and the attempt is recorded. You always know what was tried and what was stopped.

üì¶

Plugin Approval

Third-party plugins go through a review process before they can run. They're automatically scanned for security issues, and only the ones you approve get loaded into your AI assistants.

üìä

Activity Log

See everything your AI assistants have done — every tool used, every session started, every interaction. Search and filter the logs from the dashboard. Choose how much detail you want to capture.

Emergency Kill Switch

Something wrong? One click disables all AI tool access across every connected instance. If ClawForge's server goes unreachable, instances lock themselves down automatically. Safety first.

How It Works
Simple to understand, powerful to use
ClawForge connects to your OpenClaw instances and handles security and oversight behind the scenes.

Connect your AI instances

Add the ClawForge plugin to any OpenClaw instance you want to manage. It takes one config change.

Users sign in

Each user logs in with your organization's existing login system. ClawForge links them to your account automatically.

Rules are applied

Your AI instances download the latest rules from ClawForge and start following them immediately. No manual setup needed.

Everything is monitored

Tool usage, sessions, and interactions are logged and sent to ClawForge. You can view and search it all from the dashboard.

Plugins are reviewed

When someone wants to add a new plugin, it goes to you for approval. Only approved plugins are allowed to run.

You stay in control

Your AI instances check in regularly. If you flip the kill switch or update a rule, every instance picks it up within seconds.
